Making Your Presence Global: `www.sst.com`

To share this professionally crafted website with your clients and partners worldwide, it must be published online. Here's how you get `www.sst.com` live:

1. Secure Your Digital Address: The Domain Name

Your domain name, such as `www.sst.com`, is your unique identity on the internet. It's how people will find you. You acquire this by registering it annually through a **Domain Registrar** (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap). Choosing a short, memorable name like `sst.com` is ideal for brand recognition.

2. Host Your Website: Online Storage & Access

Web hosting is the service that stores your website files (HTML, CSS, images, etc.) on powerful, always-on servers connected to the internet. When someone visits your domain, the hosting server delivers your website to their browser.

  • **Hosting Providers:** Companies like Hostinger, Bluehost, SiteGround offer robust and reliable hosting solutions.
  • **Recommendation:** For a site primarily featuring static content like yours, "Shared Hosting" is an economical and efficient choice.

3. Publish Your Site: Uploading Your Files

Once you have a domain and hosting, you'll transfer your website files to the hosting server. This is typically done via two main methods:

  1. **Using Your Hosting Provider's File Manager (Recommended for Ease):**
    • Access your hosting account's **Control Panel** (often cPanel or a custom interface).
    • Locate the **"File Manager"** tool.
    • Navigate to the designated web root directory (commonly `public_html` or `www`).
    • Use the built-in upload function to transfer all your website files – including `index.html`, any CSS files (if external), and all your equipment images – into this folder.
  2. **Using an FTP (File Transfer Protocol) Client:**
    • Download and install an FTP client software (e.g., FileZilla, Cyberduck).
    • Your hosting provider will furnish you with FTP credentials (hostname, username, password).
    • Connect to your server via the FTP client, and then drag and drop your local website files into the `public_html` directory on the server.

After successfully completing these steps, your professional website will be live and accessible globally at `www.sst.com`, ready to showcase SST Salman Scientific Traders to the world!
